The Transfer Market of January

Transfer Market of January is a very important one as clubs with poor form can begin their redemption arc with new players that are performing good.


Some of the new signings:-

Cody Gakpo to Liverpool F.C

Cody Mathès Gakpo (born 7 May 1999) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays as a winger for Premier League club Liverpool and the Netherlands national team.

Joao Felix to Chelsea

João Félix Sequeira is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Chelsea, on loan from Atlético Madrid of La Liga, and the Portugal national team. He can also play as a winger or an attacking midfielder.

Cristiano Ronaldo to Al Nassr

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Professional League club Al Nassr and captains the Portugal national team

Mykhailo Mudryk to Chelsea

Mykhailo Petrovych Mudryk is an Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Premier League club Chelsea and the Ukraine national team. Mudryk began his career in the academies of Metalist Kharkiv and Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk, before moving to Shakhtar Donetsk in 2016
